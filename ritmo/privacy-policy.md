# Privacy Policy — Ritmo: Desk Wellness

**Last updated:** March 29, 2026

## Overview

Ritmo is a wellness companion app that helps desk workers maintain healthy work-break rhythms. Your privacy matters to us, and this policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data Collection

**Ritmo does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.**

All data — including session history, settings, and preferences — is stored locally on your device and never leaves it.

## Third-Party Services

Ritmo does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Data Storage

All app data is stored on your device using local storage. You can clear all your data at any time from the app's Progress screen.

## Children's Privacy

Ritmo does not collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
