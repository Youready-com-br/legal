## Frequently Asked Questions

**How does Ritmo work?**
Set your work interval and Ritmo will remind you when it's time to take a break, stretch, and move. After your break, get back to work refreshed.

**Is my data shared?**
No. All your data is stored locally on your device. Ritmo has no accounts, no cloud sync, and no tracking.

**How do I reset my progress?**
Open the Progress screen and tap "Clear progress" at the bottom.
